Multimodal museum accessibility

CAT: A Multimodal Museum Experience

Mission statement:

We are a group of four: Isaac greenwald, Evelyn Needham, Leigh Walden, and Cassidy Recker in Professour Koushik's CP341: Accessible User Interfaces course at Colorado College. This page serves as our portfolio for a final project. We partnered with the Fine Arts Center (FAC) to design a prototype that makes experiences at the FAC more accessible. Specifically, we are focusing on creating an interactive, accessible, multimodal art experience - a way to experience visual art in multiple modalities.
